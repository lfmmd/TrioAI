Remarks

Although ]P[ an ]N[ contacts may be used in LD language, it is recommended to use declared instances of R_TRIG or F_TRIG function blocks to avoid unexpected behaviour during an online change.

MyTrigger is declared as an instance of F_TRIG function block:
