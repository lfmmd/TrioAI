:= MOVE

Operator

Moves the value of a constant or variable to another variable.

Inputs

NameTypeDescription
INANYA constant value or a variable.

Outputs

NameTypeDescription
QANYA variable which will be assigned by ‘IN’.

Remarks

The data types of ‘IN’ and ‘Q’ must be the same.

ST Language

Q := -IN;

Q := - (IN1 + IN2);
